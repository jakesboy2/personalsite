About Jacob.

  I am a 24 year old developer from Oklahoma City, born and raised. I spend my time watching sports (Go Cowboys!), and I'm always thinking of a new project to dive into whether its woodworking, programming, or tinkering with electronics and hardware. When I'm not knee deep in my latest project, I'm listening to and making music or spending time with my family, friends, and lovely fiancé.

  I have a passion for computers, software and hardware, stemming back from a young age. I have gone through my early years knowing this is the path my career and talents would lead me. I will graduate with my Bachelors in Computer Science from the University of Central Oklahoma in the spring semester of 2020. I plan to have a long and fuffilling career in software development and remain absolutely estatic everyday to hone my skills and improve myself.
